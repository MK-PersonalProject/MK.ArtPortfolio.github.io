About Me

I am Mahmoud Keshtta, nice to meet you!

I am an aspiring artist from Egypt and a 10th-grade student attending Raha International School, Gardens Campus.

I've been making art for as long as I can remember, and over the years I noticed how much I've improved from the earlier stages of my journeys.

As of now, I have yet to pursue art professionally. However, I plan to continue making art even after I graduate in two years time. I love creating art of all kinds as my capabilities are very flexible. I especially enjoy making watercolor portraits and sketches using charcoal and LED.

I enjoy all mediums of art, but what resonates with me the most is stylized art, similar to that of Japanese manga. I appreciate that stylized art is a platform that allows any artist to develop their own unique art style; its a great source of creativity.

I am always looking for new opportunities to expand my skills beyond my expectations. I may still be a practicing artist, but I can't wait to see how this portfolio will grow alongside my expertise in the foreseeable future.
